Longevity Case Study

About the program

An Individualized health management and longevity program utilizing research grade data to improve health, wellness, fitness, and mental health. The program aims to collect, analyze data, and develop a program tailored to the individual's needs. The case study also seeks to bridge the gap between holistic wellness and modern medicine by uncovering the intricate interplay of various factors that contribute to a long and healthy life.

Objective- Understand desired outcome and life end goal

Strategy- Collect, Analyze Data and develop Program

Tactic: Commence Program, Modify and retest

Longevity Program Includes:

Testing

  • 3 Dexa Scan
  • 3 VO2 Max
  • 3 Resting Metabolic Rate Tests
  • 2 Blood Panels

Nutrition & Performance

  • 13 consults w/ licensed Dietitian
  • Individualized Nutrition Plan/Monitoring
  • 13 Consults with Performance Physiologist
  • Individualized training
  • Digital Profile and Program
  • Unlimited Access to Infinity HQ
  • 6 month Whoop Membership
  • Minimum 4 training sessions/week @ HQ
  • Minimum 5 movement days

Recovery

  • 12- 1:1 Recovery Sessions
  • Individualized recovery Program
